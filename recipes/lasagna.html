Lasagna

Description

This is a bitchin' lasagna recipe. Make it, eat it, and maybe share some with family.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella
  • Bolognese Sauce
  • Parsley
  1. Lay down a thin layer of Bolognese
  2. Drop a layer of noods
  3. Add Ricotta
  4. More Bolognese
  5. Moar noods
  6. Rinse and repeat
  7. Heat
  8. Eat
